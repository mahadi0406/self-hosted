<p>📲 <strong>WhatsApp Broadcasting</strong> — Send bulk campaigns via the official WhatsApp Business API with approved message templates and real-time delivery tracking.</p>

<p>✈️ <strong>Telegram Broadcasting</strong> — Broadcast messages to unlimited Telegram contacts through your own bot with Markdown formatting support.</p>

<p>🔁 <strong>Drip Sequences</strong> — Automate multi-step message sequences with custom day and hour delays to nurture leads on autopilot.</p>

<p><strong>Current Version: 1.0.0</strong></p>

<p><strong>BlastBot</strong> is a self-hosted WhatsApp and Telegram messaging platform that lets businesses reach thousands of customers from a single, clean admin panel. Built with Laravel 12 and React (Inertia.js), BlastBot combines bulk broadcasting, intelligent drip automation, AI-powered content creation, and real-time inbox management — giving marketing teams and agencies everything they need to run professional messaging campaigns at scale.</p>

<p>Install it on your own server, connect your WhatsApp Business API and Telegram bots, import your contacts, build drip sequences, and start sending. From template submission and approval tracking to Claude AI-generated campaign plans, BlastBot covers the entire messaging lifecycle — no third-party subscription fees, no per-message pricing, full control over your data.</p>

<p><strong>Own your messaging infrastructure. No recurring fees. No vendor lock-in.</strong></p>

<h3><strong>Key Highlights:</strong></h3>

<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>WhatsApp Business API Integration:</strong> Connect your official WhatsApp Business account via the Facebook Graph API. Create message templates, submit them for Meta review, sync approval status in real time, and send compliant bulk campaigns to thousands of contacts.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Telegram Bot Integration:</strong> Connect unlimited Telegram bots, receive inbound messages via webhooks, and broadcast rich Markdown-formatted messages to your entire subscriber base with a single click.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>AI Message Writer (Claude-Powered):</strong> Generate 3 ready-to-send message variants in seconds. Set your business type, goal, tone, language, emoji preference, and call-to-action — Claude AI returns compliance-scored variants ready to copy directly into your campaigns.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>AI Campaign Planner (Claude-Powered):</strong> Describe your business and goal and get a complete campaign plan back instantly — including campaign name, overview, estimated reach, expected open and reply rates, best send times, step-by-step message sequence, tips, and warnings.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Drip Sequence Automation:</strong> Build multi-step automated message flows with day and hour-level delay control. Enroll contact lists into sequences and let the scheduler send each step at exactly the right time — fully automated via background queue jobs.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>WhatsApp Template Management:</strong> Create, submit, and track WhatsApp message templates directly from the admin panel. Templates are submitted to Meta via the Graph API, and approval status (Approved / Pending / Rejected) is synced back with a single click.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Contact Management &amp; Import/Export:</strong> Manage your audience with full CRUD, CSV import, and CSV export. Organize contacts into lists, assign tags, track AI engagement labels (Hot / Warm / Cold / Dormant), and segment by status for precise targeting.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Unified Inbox:</strong> All inbound WhatsApp and Telegram replies appear in a single inbox. Messages are received via webhooks in real time, auto-classified by AI, and marked read individually or in bulk.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Campaign Analytics:</strong> Track every campaign with delivery and engagement metrics. Monitor AI tool usage, token consumption, and success rates through the dedicated AI Logs analytics page.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Background Queue Processing:</strong> Campaigns and drip steps are dispatched via Laravel queue jobs — no timeouts, no browser dependency. Supervisor keeps workers running 24/7 with automatic retries on failure.</p>
</li>
</ul>
